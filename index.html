Maku (Makanan dan Aku)
Having a healty food makes your body healthy

Problem to solve
   People don't realise their food's nutrition and for several of them who understand their nutrition needs are strugling to find the right recipe.

Product as solution
   With Maku, people can easily calculate their food's nutrition by listing the ingredients. Not only that, they can also listing the nutritions that they want and Maku will generates teh suitable recipe.

App Features
   Gizi Calculator: Calculate the food's nutrition based on the recipe.
   Recipe Finder: Generate a recipe based on the required nutrition.



Mivin, your friendly TODO app.
Pick a location, input your task and Mivin will record it for you.

Problem to solve:
   People are struggle to listing their task. Several of them tend to forget anc can't manage it.

Product as solution
   With Mivin, people can easily input their task. Not only that, they can also share their Mivin's task with a barcode to make sharing tasks more easy.

App Features
   Todo-List: Manage their task, a task is consist of title, description and location. They can edit, cross or even delete their task.
   Share and scan QR: Generate a QR which cointained their Mivin's Task to make it esier on sharing tasks.
